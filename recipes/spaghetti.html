Spaghetti

Description

This easy to make baked spaghetti recipe is sure to become a family favorite in your household!

It only takes 1h 30m to make and will serve up to eight people.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Cheeses
  • Butter

Steps

  1. 1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. 2. Cook the beef and onion together, then drain off the excess oil.
  3. 3. Add the sauce and salt. Whisk the eggs, Parmesan, and butter in a separate bowl.
  4. 4. Toss the spaghetti in the Parmesan mixture.
  5. 5. Layer the ingredients in a prepared baking dish according to the detailed recipe.
  6. 6. Cover and bake for 40 minutes. Sprinkle with mozzarella, then keep baking until the cheese is melted.
